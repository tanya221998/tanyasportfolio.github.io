# Hi, I’m Tanya 👋

I enjoy solving problems that require understanding unfamiliar systems.

Over the course of my career, I have worked across nuclear research, educational technology, food certification, and the Office of Climate and Sustainability for higher education. The industries have changed, but my approach has stayed the same: learn the system, understand the people, and build solutions that help teams make better decisions.

This portfolio collects the projects, lessons, and problem-solving patterns I have developed along the way.

## How I work

I usually start with uncertainty.

I ask what is happening, who is affected, what the constraints are, and what a useful outcome looks like. Then I research the domain, build a solution, test it in the real world, and refine it based on feedback.
